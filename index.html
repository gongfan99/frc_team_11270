About Us

A student team built at CSAA

We are Team 11270 Nova, created inside Canadian STEM & AI Academy in Markham, Ontario in the fall of 2025. Our boys team is made up of students in grades 9 through 12, and we work closely with our sister team 9785 to grow robotics opportunities and STEM involvement in our community.

Competition

The FIRST Robotics Competition

The FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) is a global program where high school teams design, build, and compete in new engineering challenges each year. Below, each season highlights our robot, events, and lessons learned.

Community

STEM matters

We promote STEM because it not only drives economic growth but also fosters rational thinking in society.

STEM for Uganda

March 14 - 15, 2026

We hosted a community book and toy sale, raising funds and collecting supplies to support a robotics student team in Uganda. The initiative helped expand access to STEM learning and hands-on robotics opportunities for local students.

STEM Outreach at CSAA March Camp

March 19, 2026

We introduced our robot and the FIRST Robotics Competition to 40 students (ages 4–14) at the CSAA March Camp, sparking curiosity and excitement for STEM through hands-on learning.
